Multiclass Search Help

Recognized search keys include:

Search keyDefinition
keyword, title, author, subject, and series support additional search subclasses, specified with a |. For example: title|proper:gone with the wind
keyword/kwsearch keyword(s)
author/au/namesearch author(s)
title/tisearch title
subject/susearch subject
series/sesearch series
langlimit by language (specify multiple langs with lang:l1 lang:l2 ...)
sitesearch at specified org unit, corresponds to actor.org_unit.shortname
sortsort type (title, author, pubdate)
dirsort direction (asc, desc)
availableif set to anything other than "false" or "0", limits to available items
